Welcome to the Marshall Electrochemical Engineering Research Lab. Lead by Professor Aaron Marshall and based in the Department of Chemical and Process Engineering at the University of Canterbury in Christchurch, New Zealand. Our research focuses on electrocatalysis and electrochemical engineering for applications in green energy, chemicals and metal production processes.

Research Interests

  • Electrochemical Energy Technology
  • Flow batteries
  • Electrocatalytic carbon dioxide reduction
  • Water electrolysis
  • Recovery and Extraction of Critical Minerals
  • Ultra-high temperature electrochemistry
