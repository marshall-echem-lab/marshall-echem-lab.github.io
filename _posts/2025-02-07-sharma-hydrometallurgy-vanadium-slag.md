---
title: Shailendra's paper on vanadium recovery from slag in Hydrometallurgy
categories: [news]
---

Congratulations to Shailendra Sharma on his <a href="https://doi.org/10.1016/j.hydromet.2024.106433" target="_blank">paper</a> in Hydrometallurgy on the recovery of vanadium from industrial slag. The study investigates leaching and processing strategies for extracting vanadium from waste metallurgical residues, with relevance to domestic vanadium supply chains for flow battery applications. Great to continue collaborating with Chris Bumby on this one, along with Megan Girdwood, Mohammad Arif, and colleagues. Well done Shailendra!
