Congratulations to Shailendra Sharma on his <a href="https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0360319924057045" target="_blank">paper</a> in the International Journal of Hydrogen Energy investigating ruthenium-based electrocatalysts for the oxygen evolution reaction. The study examines catalyst activity, stability, and structure–performance relationships relevant to proton exchange membrane water electrolysis. A large collaborative effort involving Chao Wu, Laura Titheridge, John Kennedy, Vesna Jovic, and colleagues. Well done Shailendra!
