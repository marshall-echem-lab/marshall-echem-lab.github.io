---
title: Pitambar's computational paper on decomposition of heterocycles
categories: [news]
---

Congratulations to Pitambar Poudel on his <a href="https://doi.org/10.1016/j.comptc.2024.114749" target="_blank">paper</a> in Computational and Theoretical Chemistry using DFT calculations to predict the thermal decomposition temperatures of heterocyclic compounds relevant to carbon synthesis. The computational approach provides useful guidance for selecting and tuning precursors for heteroatom-doped carbon materials. Great work in collaboration with Alison Masters. Well done Pitambar!
