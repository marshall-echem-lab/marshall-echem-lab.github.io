---
title: Sophie's paper on ion beam modification of carbon electrodes in Journal of Power Sources
categories: [news]
---

Congratulations to Sophie McArdle on her <a href="https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jpowsour.2024.234614" target="_blank">paper</a> in the Journal of Power Sources on ion beam modification of carbon electrodes. The study examines how ion beam treatment alters the surface chemistry and electrochemical activity of carbon, with relevance to improving electrode performance in flow batteries. A great collaborative effort with Axel Fiedler, Julien Leveneur, and John Kennedy. Well done Sophie!
