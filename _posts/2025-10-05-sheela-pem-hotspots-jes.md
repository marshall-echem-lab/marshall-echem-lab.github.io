title: Sheela's paper on temperature hotspots in PEM electrolysers in the Journal of The Electrochemical Society
categories: [news]
---

Congratulations to Sheela on her <a href="https://doi.org/10.1149/1945-7111/ae0fe9" target="_blank">paper</a> in the Journal of The Electrochemical Society investigating temperature hotspot formation in proton exchange membrane water electrolysers. Understanding and managing local thermal gradients is critical for improving the durability and efficiency of PEM electrolysers at scale. A collaborative effort with Jae Woo, John Kennedy, and Yucheng Liu. Well done Sheela!
