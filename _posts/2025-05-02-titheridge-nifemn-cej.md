title: Laura's paper on NiFeMn catalysts for oxygen evolution in Chemical Engineering Journal
categories: [news]
---

Congratulations to Laura Titheridge on her <a href="https://doi.org/10.1016/j.cej.2025.162322" target="_blank">paper</a> in the Chemical Engineering Journal investigating NiFeMn electrocatalysts for the oxygen evolution reaction. The work examines how manganese incorporation into NiFe-based materials affects OER activity and stability, contributing to the design of improved earth-abundant catalysts for alkaline and anion exchange membrane electrolysers. Great work with Chao Wu, Shailendra Sharma, and the wider team. Well done Laura!
