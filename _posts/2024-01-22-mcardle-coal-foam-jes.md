---
title: Sophie's paper on coal-derived carbon foam electrodes for vanadium flow batteries
categories: [news]
---

Congratulations to Sophie McArdle on her <a href="https://doi.org/10.1149/1945-7111/ad2591" target="_blank">paper</a> in the Journal of The Electrochemical Society exploring coal-derived carbon foam as a low-cost electrode material for vanadium redox flow batteries. The study demonstrates that coal-derived foams can deliver competitive electrochemical performance, offering a potentially scalable route to affordable electrode materials for grid-scale energy storage. A great collaborative effort with Minh Dang and Nick Holland. Well done Sophie!
