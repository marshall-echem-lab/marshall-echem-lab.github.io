---
title: New paper on a copper molecular electrocatalyst for hydrogen evolution
categories: [news]
---

We have a new <a href="https://doi.org/10.1039/D4DT00224E" target="_blank">paper</a> in Dalton Transactions on a copper complex that forms an active and stable electrocatalyst for hydrogen evolution in neutral water. The work was led by Abdullah Abudayyeh and Johan Hamonnet in collaboration with Sally Brooker's group in the Chemistry department at UC, and shows that a square planar Cu(II) complex deposits a catalytically active copper/copper oxide film on the electrode surface during electrolysis. A great piece of cross-disciplinary chemistry and electrochemistry.
