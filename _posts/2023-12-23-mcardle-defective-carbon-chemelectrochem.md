---
title: Sophie's review on defective carbon for sodium-ion and vanadium flow batteries
categories: [news]
---

Congratulations to Sophie McArdle on her <a href="https://doi.org/10.1002/celc.202300512" target="_blank">review</a> in ChemElectroChem covering defective carbon materials for next-generation stationary energy storage across sodium-ion batteries and vanadium redox flow batteries. The review maps defect engineering strategies and their effects on electrode performance, providing a comprehensive guide for the field. A collaborative effort with Fabian Bauer and the group of Fabio Di Fonzo at IIT Milan.
