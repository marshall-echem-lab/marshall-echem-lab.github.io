---
title: Shailendra's paper on Nafion ionomer effects in Au cluster CO2 electroreduction
categories: [news]
---

Congratulations to Shailendra Sharma on his <a href="https://doi.org/10.1149/1945-7111/ace12e" target="_blank">paper</a> in the Journal of The Electrochemical Society investigating the role of Nafion ionomer in the electrochemical CO2 reduction performance of gold cluster-based electrodes. The study shows that Nafion content significantly affects the activity and selectivity of Au cluster catalysts, with implications for designing better CO2 electroreduction electrodes. Another great piece of work in collaboration with Bernt Johannessen and Vladimir Golovko. Well done Shailendra!
