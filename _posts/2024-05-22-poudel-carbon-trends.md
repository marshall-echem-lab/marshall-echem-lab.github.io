---
title: Pitambar's paper on oxygen-functionalized carbons from heterocyclic pyrolysis in Carbon Trends
categories: [news]
---

Congratulations to Pitambar Poudel on his <a href="https://doi.org/10.1016/j.cartre.2024.100333" target="_blank">paper</a> in Carbon Trends investigating the synthesis of oxygen-functionalized carbon materials through the pyrolysis of heterocyclic precursors. The work explores how heteroatom-containing organic precursors can be used to tune the surface chemistry and electrochemical properties of the resulting carbons. A nice two-author collaboration — well done Pitambar!
