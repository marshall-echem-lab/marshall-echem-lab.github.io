title: Laura's review on anion exchange membrane water electrolysis in Current Opinion in Electrochemistry
categories: [news]
---

Congratulations to Laura Titheridge on her <a href="https://doi.org/10.1016/j.coelec.2024.101607" target="_blank">review</a> in Current Opinion in Electrochemistry covering anion exchange membrane (AEM) water electrolysis. The review provides a timely overview of the current status, challenges, and opportunities for AEM electrolysers as a lower-cost alternative to PEM technology for green hydrogen production. A collaborative effort with Shailendra Sharma and colleagues. Well done Laura!
