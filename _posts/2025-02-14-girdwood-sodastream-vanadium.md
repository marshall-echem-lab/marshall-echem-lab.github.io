title: Megan's paper on CO2-assisted vanadium leaching in ACS Sustainable Chemistry & Engineering
categories: [news]
---

Congratulations to Megan Girdwood on her <a href="https://doi.org/10.1021/acssuschemeng.4c08294" target="_blank">paper</a> in ACS Sustainable Chemistry & Engineering describing a novel CO<sub>2</sub>-assisted leaching process for vanadium extraction — using a SodaStream device to generate carbonic acid as the lixiviant! The approach offers a low-cost, low-hazard route to vanadium recovery from industrial residues. Great to continue collaborating with Chris Bumby on this work, along with Mohammad Arif, Lisa Pearcy, and Kathryn Dahm. A creative and fun study — well done Megan!
